FAQ

Frequently
Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your stay at Villa Mira Kalkan.

The Villa
Where is Villa Mira located?

Villa Mira is nestled in the green hills above Kalkan, a charming coastal town on Turkey's Turquoise Coast — approximately 20 km from Kaş and 80 km from Antalya Airport.

How many guests can the villa accommodate?

The villa accommodates up to 8 guests across 4 spacious en-suite bedrooms.

Is the pool heated?

Yes. The private infinity pool is heated and open from sunrise. It offers panoramic views across Kalkan Bay and the Aegean Sea.

Is there parking at the villa?

Yes. There is private gated parking for two vehicles, monitored 24/7.

Is WiFi available?

Yes. High-speed fibre internet is available throughout the villa, including all terraces.

Are pets allowed?

Pets are not permitted at Villa Mira, to maintain a peaceful and clean environment for all guests.

Booking & Stay
What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 15:00 and check-out is by 11:00. Early or late arrangements may be possible upon request.

What is the minimum stay?

The minimum stay is 7 nights during peak season. Shorter stays may be considered in off-peak periods — please enquire directly.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation terms depend on the booking platform or direct rental agreement. Please contact us for full details before confirming your reservation.

Can a private chef be arranged?

Yes. A private chef can be arranged on request through our concierge service. Please mention this when making your enquiry.

How do I make a reservation?

Check availability on our homepage calendar and send an enquiry via the contact form, email, or WhatsApp. We reply within 24 hours.

Around Kalkan
What is there to do near the villa?

Kalkan offers boat trips, scuba diving, water sports, and excellent seafood restaurants. Nearby highlights include Kaputaş Beach (10 km), the ancient Lycian city of Xanthos, and the stunning Saklikent Gorge. The old town's cobbled streets and rooftop terraces are perfect for evening dining.

How far is the nearest beach?

Kalkan's beach club platforms are a short drive down to the harbour. The iconic Kaputaş Beach — one of Turkey's most beautiful — is approximately 10 km away. Boat trips to secluded coves can also be arranged.
